For those of you who handload, I've started a free weekly newsletter called The Load Bench (theloadbench.com). Every week it covers component availability from the major online suppliers — Powder Valley, Graf's, Midsouth, Widener's — so you know what's in stock before you make a trip or place an order. There's also a load data section focused on the cartridges most of us shoot: 6GT, .308, .223, 9mm, and .45 ACP. No beginner tutorials, no fluff — just useful information for people who actually reload.

It's completely free. If you're interested, subscribe at theloadbench.com or join the Discord community at discord.gg/BS62QQufQJ.
