Trusted Suppliers

Resources

Where to buy components online. These are the suppliers I actually use and check weekly for the newsletter. Some links are affiliate links — costs you nothing, helps keep the newsletter free.

Components — Powder, Primers & Brass

Powder Valley

powdervalleyinc.com

Best selection of powder and primers. First place to check for H4350, Varget, and Federal match primers. Restocks frequently on Friday mornings.

Midsouth Shooters Supply

midsouthshooterssupply.com

Wide selection of components including 6GT brass. Good prices on bulk primers and powder. Worth checking when Powder Valley is out.

Graf & Sons

grafs.com

Best source for Vihtavuori powders (N140, N150, N160). Also carries Lapua and Peterson brass. Reliable stock on European powders.

Widener's

wideners.com

Most consistent Varget supply. Good for bulk .308 and .223 components. Worth bookmarking if you go through a lot of Hodgdon powder.

Lucky Gunner

luckygunner.com

Good for bulk brass — 9mm, .45 ACP, .223. Reliable shipping. Useful for pistol reloaders stocking up on high-volume cartridges.

Brownells

brownells.com

Bullets, dies, presses, and reloading equipment. Largest selection of reloading tools. Good for Berger bullets and precision components.

Bullets

Berger Bullets

berger-bullets.com

105gr Hybrid and 108gr BT for 6GT. 175gr OTM for .308. The standard for precision rifle competition. Buy direct when in stock.

Sierra Bullets

sierrabullets.com

175gr MatchKing — the gold standard for .308 precision work. 77gr for .223. Consistent quality, well-documented BCs.

Equipment & Tools

RCBS

rcbs.com

Presses, dies, case prep tools. The Rock Chucker is a workhorse for precision single-stage loading. Well-supported, parts available everywhere.

Redding Reloading

redding-reloading.com

Premium dies for precision work. Competition seating die is the standard for serious loaders. Type S micrometer dies for repeatable seating depth.

Premium Brass

Lapua

The benchmark for .308 and .338 brass. Extremely consistent weight and neck thickness.

Peterson Brass

Best option for 6GT. More consistent neck tension than Lapua in this cartridge. American-made.

Alpha Munitions

Premium 6GT brass. Excellent quality control. Sells out fast — buy when available.
